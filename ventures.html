The Opportunity
Problem
Millions of Nigerians with prepaid electricity meters go dark when they run out of credit — due to irregular income, salary delays, or emergencies. No immediate cash means no power, and expensive alternatives like generators fill the gap.
Solution
uSwitch is a fintech platform that modernises how Nigerians access prepaid electricity — combining instant token purchase, a Buy Now Pay Later credit facility, and automated recurring top-ups so users never go dark again.
Core Product
⚡
Instant Token Purchase
Buy electricity tokens immediately via card, bank transfer, or wallet — any time, any amount.
💳
Buy Now, Pay Later
Access ₦3,000–₦20,000 in electricity credit repayable in 7–14 days at a flat service fee. Mandatory auto-debit ensures repayment and manages default risk.
🔁
Auto Top-Up
Set weekly or monthly auto-purchase schedules. Predictable power with zero manual effort.
Why Now
📈
Prepaid meter adoption is high and growing across Nigeria — the infrastructure base is already there.
🥇
BNPL is proven in retail but untested in utilities — uSwitch has a first-mover advantage in this space.
🏗️
Payment gateways, KYC providers, and aggregators now exist to support rapid scaling in Nigeria.
🎯
Users are price-aware and actively seeking solutions to power reliability — demand is latent and immediate.
